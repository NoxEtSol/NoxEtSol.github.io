About me

Hey, I'm Adam. {Lifelong Learner & Explorer}

My journey into IT wasn't a straight path. I started as a chef, then worked as a bartender, barista, shop manager, and team leader. Each role shaped me, teaching me adaptability, problem-solving, and the importance of working with people.

Over time, I realized something was missing. I wanted to build, create, and challenge myself in new ways. That’s when I took a leap into the world of development. I joined Greenfox Academy, where I built a strong foundation in Java and backend technologies. Since then, I’ve been expanding my knowledge in Spring, Quarkus, Microservices, Monoliths / Moduliths, distributed systems, Kafka, CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes, Security, DevOps practices, and more.

Today, I love diving into complex problems, both in code and in life. Not only learning new technologies, but also seeking wisdom in each step of life is my passion. I may not know everything yet, but I’m always eager to grow, explore, and improve with each challenge.
